A family run party and snack food provider offering frozen food delivery. We are following Government guidelines keeping our customers Covid secure.

Que Chevere is a frozen food service supplier that is small enough to care yet big enough to cope. Personal service, reliability, quality, innovation and value are the watchwords which have enabled us to offer a product and distribution facility that allows us to meet our customer requirements easily and without fuss.

Our main product is:

Frozen party food and snack food in the form of a delicacy called tequeños which are small cheese sticks, rolled in soft dough and fried to a crunchy and delicious perfection. They are fantastically simple and quick to prepare and provide an exciting dip alternative.

Tequeños are the new tapa that is causing a sensation in America and Spain and we present it to you in three versions: mozzarella cheese, Chorizo and Iberico Ham (Serrano).

We dispatch our product, ensuring the highest quality and hygiene standards across our stock and delivery.
